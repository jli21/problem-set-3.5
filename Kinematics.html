Calculus and it's application in Physics

Calculus has numerous applications, particularly in physics. Calculus is used in fields such as kinematics, thermodynamics, and rotation. From the creation of calculus by Newton in the mid 17th century, caclulus has continued to develop and change the way we view the natural world. Physics is defined as the natural science that studies matter and its motion and behavior throughout time and space.
